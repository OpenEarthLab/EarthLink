Evaluate the performance of CMIP6 models in simulating surface temperature.
Requirements:
1. Analyze the annual mean and seasonal cycle in different regions and plot time series
2. Give the model a ranking, which can be displayed in a bar chart.
3. CMIP6 models: ACCESS-CM2, ACCESS-ESM1-5, BCC-CSM2-MR, CAMS-CSM1-0, CAS-ESM2-0, CESM2-WACCM, CMCC-CM2-SR5, CMCC-ESM2, CanESM5, E3SM-2-0, FGOALS-f3-L, GFDL-ESM4, INM-CM4-8, INM-CM5-0, IPSL-CM6A-LR, IPSL-CM5A2-INCA, KACE-1-0-G, MIROC6, MPI-ESM1-2-LR, MRI-ESM2-0, TaiESM1
4. Reference dataset: ERA5.
5. Time period: 1985-2014.
