Evaluate the performance of CMIP6 models in simulating sea ice concentration.
Requirements:
1. Analyze the statistical characteristics of variables, including climatology, standard deviation, etc.
2. Use the polar projection in 'cartopy' package for map plot.
3. Give the model a ranking, which can be displayed in a bar chart.
4. CMIP6 models: ACCESS-ESM1-5, BCC-CSM2-MR, BCC-ESM1, CAMS-CSM1-0, CESM2, CESM2-FV2, CESM2-WACCM, CESM2-WACCM-FV2, CMCC-CM2-HR4, CMCC-ESM2, CanESM5, FGOALS-f3-L, FGOALS-g3, FIO-ESM-2-0, GFDL-CM4, GFDL-ESM4, GISS-E2-1-H, GISS-E2-2-H, INM-CM4-8, INM-CM5-0, IPSL-CM5A2-INCA, IPSL-CM6A-LR-INCA, KIOST-ESM, MIROC6, MPI-ESM-1-2-HAM, MPI-ESM1-2-LR, NESM3, NorCPM1, TaiESM1
5. Reference thetao Dataset: HadISST.
6. Time period: 1985-2014.
