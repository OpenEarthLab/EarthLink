1. CMIP6 models: ACCESS-CM2, ACCESS-ESM1-5, BCC-CSM2-MR, BCC-ESM1, CAMS-CSM1-0, CAS-ESM2-0, CESM2, CESM2-FV2, CESM2-WACCM, CESM2-WACCM-FV2, CIESM, CMCC-CM2-HR4, CMCC-CM2-SR5, CMCC-ESM2, CanESM5, E3SM-1-0, E3SM-2-0, FGOALS-f3-L, FIO-ESM-2-0, GISS-E2-1-G, GISS-E2-1-G-CC, GISS-E2-1-H, GISS-E2-2-G, GISS-E2-2-H, INM-CM4-8, INM-CM5-0, IPSL-CM5A2-INCA, IPSL-CM6A-LR, IPSL-CM6A-LR-INCA, KACE-1-0-G, KIOST-ESM, MIROC6, MPI-ESM-1-2-HAM, MPI-ESM1-2-LR, NESM3, NorCPM1, TaiESM1
2. Rank models by their TCR values and give the multi-model mean, and plot a PDF distribution diagram.
