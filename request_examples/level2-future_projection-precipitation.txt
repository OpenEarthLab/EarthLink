Analyze global average precipitation changes under different SSP scenarios
Requirements:
1. CMIP6 models: CAMS-CSM1-0, CanESM5, FGOALS-g3, GFDL-ESM4, IPSL-CM6A-LR, MIROC6, MPI-ESM1-2-LR, MRI-ESM2-0.
2. Use 1985–2014 as the historical baseline for anomalies.
3. Plot:
   - Time series of global average precipitation anomalies for each scenario.
   - Bar chart comparing projected warming in 2050–2099 across scenarios.
