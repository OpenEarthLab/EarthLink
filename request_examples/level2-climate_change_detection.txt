Detect the presence of anthropogenic climate change signals in the global average temperature from 1901 to 2015.
Requirements:
1. Use the fingerprint detection method.
2. Include comparison of different forcing factors and plot the anomaly time series in different forcing factors.
3. CMIP6 model: ACCESS-CM2, ACCESS-ESM1-5, BCC-CSM2-MR, CanESM5, GFDL-ESM4, IPSL-CM6A-LR, MRI-ESM2-0 
4. Reference Dataset: HadCRUT5
